UpScale Marketing Solutions Privacy Statement

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. The personal information we collect allows us to support your account and run your promotion campaigns. We guarantee that we only use the information for supporting your account and running your promotion campaigns.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

Below is the list of information we collect about you, your devices and your videos:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • When you create a promotion campaign we save the URL of the video/videos you have entered, and URL of the YouTube channel you have entered or we found using the video's URL
  • We collect the public information about your YouTube video (its title, number of views, likes, dislikes, comments, the length of the video)
  • YouTube channel information (its URL, number of subscribers)

Information Sharing and Protection

We don't share the information you provide us with anyone else without your consent.

🔐 Your Data Security

The information is stored in secure servers. We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of your personal data, and to protect it against accidental loss, destruction or damage.

The data you provide to us is protected using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology. SSL is the industry standard method of encrypting personal information and credit card details so that they can be securely transferred over the Internet.

Data Access and Management

Only our employees and web developers have access to your data. And only to the data which is necessary for their job. For example, your support manager will have access to your contact data such as your email address and the list of your videos.

Age Restrictions

We do not allow users under the age of 18 to use our services. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone who is under the age of 18.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us through our website at https://upscalemarketingsolutions.com.
